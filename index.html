About Me

I am a photographer and videographer based in the heart of Cannes. My work focuses on reportage, intimate portraits, and cinematic travel journals. I believe that the most powerful images are the ones that feel unscripted and authentic.

Whether capturing a wedding on the Côte d’Azur, a commercial campaign, or a personal travel story, I chase the natural light and the honest moments in between poses.

Available for: Local projects in France and destination assignments worldwide.
